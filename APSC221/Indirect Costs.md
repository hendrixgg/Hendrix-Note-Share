Costs that are difficult to allocate to a specific output or work activity.

examples:
- common tools
- general supplies
- equipment maintenance

(Overhead specific):
- plant operating costs that are not direct labor or direct material costs.
- electricity
- general repairs
- property taxes
- supervision
